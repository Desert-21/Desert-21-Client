About game...

Desert 21 is a completely free to play indie strategic game. Check "How to Play" to understand the rules - might feel overwhelming at the beginning, but it's actually simpler than it looks like. Why would you even want to try it? Here's a few reasons:

  • It is completely free to play
  • Pay 2 win is not (and will never be) a thing here
  • It won't make you check your phone 24/7 - when the game ends, it ends
  • RNG (randomness) is not that much pain
  • It's based on turns - not on quick reactions or having a script that clicks stuff every five minutes. Just pure strategy
  • Addictive as ****
